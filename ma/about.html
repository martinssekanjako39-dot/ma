SCHOOL PROFILE

Brain bunch Nursery and Primary school was started on 1st january 2022 in Makenke town in Mbarara city.

The school was started by MR.MUWONGE MUHAMAD under islamic set up to teach the young generation both islamic religion and formal education

The school is a bording and day privately owned. It is registered by Ministry of education and sports

The school teaches all young generation ranges from nursery one to primary seven. it teaches islamic religion to pupils but all religions are welcomed to our school
